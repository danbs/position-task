Marriam-Webster defines a company as an association of persons for carrying on a commercial or industrial enterprise. HR has more to do with the persons aspect of business. It is important to understand how to manage every part of the process/business because people are at every part of the process of doing business. As a human resource representative, you will be making lots of decisions for the companies you work for. In this case, you will make almost all the decisions for your company as you will have the opportunity of making your own company because the rich uncle you never knew had left you money. Your uncle ran away from home when he was young and became a successful business man. He wants to see if you would be a good successor to his very profitable business by giving you money. You have 100 million dollars to start your own global company. If you’re company is not successful, your uncle says that the company will be sold and he will find someone else to take over his business and he will start an ad campaign that will tell everyone not to hire you as an HR person.

You will need to design an organization chart for their company. Your uncle gave you the following information to start and he wants to see a well-thought-out organizational chart.
Industry: Technology
Product/service: Website hosting
Corporate departments:

  1. Sales and marketing
  2. Quality assurance
  3. Finance
  4. HR
  5. IT
In this assignment you will be learning about organizational structure so you can better apply it to the business you are making. Different styles of business have different structures that they use so you should know about all of them to be better at making decisions.

You will have looked at or read or watched the resources provided above, write up your companies’ org chart. It can be rough since you will be developing it farther, but it is important that you have a rough idea of the org for your uncle asap.
